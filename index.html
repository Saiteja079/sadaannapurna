ABOUT — SADA ANNAPURNA

Hello,Our food donation app strives to create a seamless platform that harnesses technology to bridge the gap between surplus food and those in need. By fostering a community-driven approach, we aim to minimize food waste while maximizing support for individuals and organizations combating hunger. Our vision is to create a world where no edible food goes to waste and where every person has access to nourishing meals, building a more sustainable and compassionate society.

How it all started

From the spark of an idea to the realization of a dream, the journey of Acme began with a single moment of inspiration and a fierce determination to succeed.

Our approach

Our food donation platform leverages technology to connect surplus food with those in need. Through community-driven efforts, we reduce food waste and support hunger relief. Our vision: a world where no edible food is wasted, ensuring nourishment for all.
